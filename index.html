In the past, I was currently a senior engineer in Tencent. My work spans two fields. One part is in the back-end software development of the advertising system, and the other part is focusing on recommender system for online advertising platform, especially in user interest modeling, CTR prediction. Prior to that, I worked as a software engineer at JD Inc . from July 2018 to November 2019. I designed a genetic algorithm for delivery tasks, optimizing batch allocation and routes, reducing cost and run time. Also, I developed a distributed order task system for the 11.11 festival and a custom order protocol, saving storage costs. I received a double bachelor's degree in Telecommunication Engineering / Marine Technology from Ocean University of China (OUC) in June 2018.

My past research experience includes data mining and recommendation system. But I am also very interested in Agentic Systems, Data-centric AI, and large language models(LLM). I truly believe that these will bring new opportunities and changes to the world and people, especially the residents in poor areas.
